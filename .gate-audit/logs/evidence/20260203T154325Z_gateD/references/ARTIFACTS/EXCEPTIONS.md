# EXCEPTIONS

監査（Gate D）や運用上の理由により、通常ルールから一時的に外した内容を記録します。
（例：期限都合で一部Evidenceを後追い、既存資産の制約で命名を一部維持、など）

---

## Exception Format
- ID: `EXC-YYYYMMDD-###`
- Date: `<YYYY-MM-DD>`
- Scope: `<what is affected>`
- Reason: `<why exception is needed>`
- Risk: `<risk introduced>`
- Mitigation: `<how risk is reduced>`
- Evidence: `<links/paths/logs>`
- Owner: `<who approved>`
- Expiry: `<date or condition to remove exception>`
- Re-audit condition: `<what must be true to close>`
